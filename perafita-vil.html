Perafita Village Garden - UX/UI Design

The Perafita Village Garden or Perafita Tool Share is a individual project that I started after moving into a closed condominium and realizing that having each family buy their individual tools would be an unnecessary waste of money and resources.

The idea was to create an app that would help the residents manage tools that'd be available to the whole community, in which they would only have to reserve determined tools at a determined time through the app.
