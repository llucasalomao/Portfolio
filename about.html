Who am I?

I'm a college student in the arts who studied socioeconomic sciences in high school. I'm a shy person who likes to express himself through art, mainly through photography and the music I listen to.

Why the sudden change of study area?

I have always had photography as one of my favorite hobbies and I had never thought of following this path as my career. But after 3 years studying economics in high school, I realized that it wasn't what I wanted to do, so when I found the course I'm on today, I was sure it was the right path for me.

What do I do in my free time?

As I already said, I love photography and music, but they are not the only activities I like to do. I've always really enjoyed painting pictures, sewing, playing sports and like any young person, I love spending time with my closest friends.

What sports have I played?

Currently I practice 4 sports: Football, Futsal, Handball and Basketball, but when I was younger I trained Judo and Rugby.

What kind of music do I listen to?

I would say that I love music in general, but my favorite genres are rock, pop and alternative-rap/emo-rap. Some of my favorite artists are Lil Peep, Queen and Taylor Swift.

What was my inspiration to start taking pictures?

Ever since I can remember, I've watched my father's love for photography and I'll never forget the day that he lent me one of his cameras to help him shoot a family event and I've loved photography ever since.

What is my favorite photography style?

That depends. If we're talking about my favorite pictures to take, I would have to say probably landscape, something inspired by the group F/64. If we're talking about something I really want to do in the future, I would say probably sports like Basketball or Formula 1 and Fashion.

What do I want for my future?

When I finish my degree, I intend to do a master's degree in Fashion Design and my goal is to one day be able to combine my knowledge from the degree with the master's to create my own clothing brand.
