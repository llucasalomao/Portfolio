Licor Pato - Fictional Add

Licor Pato was one of the first assignments that I develop as a group project. As there was no theme or concept we decided to make a fake add for a personalized liquor brand, Licor Pato (Duck Liquor). In this project I not only acted as the model but also assisted at production and post production.
